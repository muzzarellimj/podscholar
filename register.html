Register

Register an account with PodScholar to gain access to content curation, the ability to like and bookmark content, and a distinct user profile to share with the world.

  1. Register

    First, enter an email and password to register with.

  2. Profile

    Next, provide us with your first and last name.

    Then, choose a username. It must be unique!

    Finally, upload an avatar. This is optional.

  3. Creator

    Question: would you also like to register as a creator?

    Great! First, provide us with your current affiliation.

    Next, provide a short description about yourself.

  4. Complete

    Welcome to PodScholar! You should be receiving an email with an account verification link. Feel free to take a look around, and if you have any questions, reach out!
